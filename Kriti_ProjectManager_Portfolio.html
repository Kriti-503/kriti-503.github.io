AI EdTech Programme Delivery Framework
A structured end-to-end delivery framework for AI-integrated academic programmes — designed to solve the operational chaos observed managing cohorts across IIM and IIT institutions, where fragmented communication and reactive decision-making were degrading learner experience and NPS.
Type
Delivery Framework
Domain
AI EdTech / Ops
Role
Project Manager
Method
Agile / Scrum
Status
Completed 2025
Phase 01
Initiation & Planning
  • Defined project charter: objectives, stakeholders, timeline, KPIs
  • Mapped work breakdown structure (WBS) across onboarding, delivery, and closure phases
  • Identified risks: faculty availability, AI tool adoption friction, learner drop-off
  • Success criteria: NPS ≥ 8, completion rate ≥ 85%, issue resolution ≤ 48hrs
Phase 02
Sprint Execution
  • Created sprint plan: 2-week cycles with defined reviews and retrospective checkpoints
  • Ran weekly standups with faculty, tech, and learner support teams
  • Managed Jira board with epics for onboarding, content delivery, and feedback loops
  • Tracked velocity and backlog health; escalated blockers within 24hrs
Phase 03
Monitoring & Control
  • Tracked NPS and engagement metrics cohort-by-cohort
  • Coordinated AI tool rollout with platform team — 3-week integration plan
  • Delivered bi-weekly leadership dashboards with programme health status
  • Managed scope creep: deferred 3 features via backlog grooming
Phase 04
Closure & Review
  • Ran retrospective: identified top 3 friction points, documented solutions
  • Produced SOP documentation for repeatable delivery across future cohorts
  • Presented data-backed improvement roadmap to leadership
  • Framework adopted as template for all subsequent programme cohorts
